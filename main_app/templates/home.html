How FeastMode works:

FeastMode takes the stress out of organizing a potluck by making the process seamless and user-friendly. Here’s how it works:

  • Create a Party – The host sets up the potluck event in the app and gets a unique party code.
  • Send the Code – Share the code with guests so they can easily join the party.
  • Sign Up & RSVP – Guests can confirm their attendance and see what’s needed.
  • Claim or Add a Dish – The host can propose dishes, and guests can claim an option or add their own contribution.
  • Describe Your Dish – Guests can enter details about their dish, including ingredients and allergens, so everyone knows what to expect.
  • Enjoy a Stress-Free Potluck – With everything organized in one place, you can focus on the fun, not the logistics!
